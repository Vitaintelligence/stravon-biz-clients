## ðŸ”¥ Corporate Hacks Explained

### 1. **The "7 Days" Hook**
Most agencies say "fast" but don't specify. We say exactly "7 days" which:
- Creates a specific expectation
- Sounds achievable (not too good to be true)
- Easy to remember and share

### 2. **Fixed Pricing Strategy**
Instead of "Contact for quote":
- Shows confidence in process
- Removes friction (no sales call needed)
- Builds trust (no hidden costs)
- Qualifies leads automatically

### 3. **WhatsApp Over Forms**
Why WhatsApp works better:
- Lower friction (they already have it)
- Instant response expectation
- More personal feel
- Higher response rate
- Pre-filled message reduces effort

### 4. **The "While Others..." Pattern**
Every section contrasts with competitors:
- "While others take months..."
- "Traditional agencies charge..."
- "They're stuck in outdated processes..."

This positions you as the obvious choice.

### 5. **Stat Stacking**
Multiple impressive numbers:
- 7 days delivery
- â‚¹50-80k pricing
- 10x faster
- 50+ apps
- 100% satisfaction

Creates overwhelming evidence of value.
